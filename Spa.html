Massage

Summary of massage facilitys

Experience the ultimate in relaxation with our range of soothing massages, specially designed for couples to ease tension and promote overall well-being. Our skilled therapists use a variety of techniques tailored to your individual needs, ensuring a personalized experience that leaves you both feeling rejuvenated.

To ensure you receive the best possible service, we recommend booking your massage at least 24 hours in advance, as availability is limited.

Massage Packages:

  • 30-Minute Relaxation Massage: A quick treatment focusing on key tension areas to help you unwind together.
  • 45-Minute Deep Tissue Massage: Targets deeper muscle layers to relieve tension and enhance relaxation for both partners.
  • 1-Hour Signature Couples Massage: Indulge in a blissful experience designed to harmonize your senses and deepen your connection, making it an unforgettable part of your getaway.

Couples Resort
Sauna

Summary of Sauna facilities

Step into a world of tranquility and rejuvenation in the luxurious sauna facilities at Couples Resort, designed to provide the ultimate relaxation experience for couples. Here, you can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse yourselves in soothing heat that promotes detoxification, relaxation, and an overall sense of well-being—perfect for enhancing your romantic getaway.

We offer two distinct sauna options to cater to your needs. Our Standard Sauna comfortably accommodates up to four people, providing an intimate setting for couples or small groups. With warm wooden interiors and gentle steam, this sauna creates a serene atmosphere where you can unwind together. As you relax in the calming heat, let the stresses of everyday life melt away, allowing you to reconnect and enjoy each other's company in a peaceful environment.

For those seeking a more spacious experience, our Deluxe Sauna can accommodate up to six people. This larger sauna features additional seating and enhanced amenities, making it ideal for couples wanting to share the experience with friends or family. The invigorating heat combined with a soothing ambiance creates an inviting space for relaxation and rejuvenation. Whether you choose the Standard or Deluxe option, our saunas provide a perfect escape for self-care and quality time together.

Summary of Wellness offerings
